The syntax of my language in Backus-Naur Form

<while> ::= "while " <comparison>
<comparison> ::= <addition> " " <comparator> " " <addition> 

<print> ::= "print " <addition>
<assignment> ::= <variable> " = " <addition> 
<addition> ::= <term> | <term> " + " <term>
<term> ::= <number> | <variable>

<number> ::= [1-9][0-9]*
<variable> ::= [A-Za-z_]+
<comparator> ::= "<" | "==" | ">"
